Visit Kamurocho

Enjoy the nightlife capital of Japan. The city has charm and character everywhere you look, to the point you will feel like Kamurocho is coming alive in a way!

Top three activities to do at Kamurocho

Millenium Tower

Even though the Millenium Tower explodes every other day, the sick ass fights that happen make it all worth it. You might even get a nice helicopter ride with our one and only Date-san! Fun fact: I don't know how they repair it so nicely every single time.

Pocket Circuit Racing

Believe me, it is not a "only for kids" thing. Things here are taken seriously, by kids and men. You might beat some little dude that will certainly call you a virgin but at the same time find you cool because of your skills - if you even have any.

The Coliseum

The crown of the Purgatory! Opened after the disgraced former police detective known as The Florist of Sai went underground to become a private information dealer. You can see some cool fights, but not like in every other corner of Kamurocho. Here the stakes are higher and even you can participate for a certain fee.
